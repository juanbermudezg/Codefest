Team's description

The team is composed of students and analysts from Universidad Nacional de Colombia and Escuela de Aviación del Ejército, with a shared passion for data science and the use of technology to solve complex problems. Their objective is to apply their programming and data analysis knowledge to the recognition of satellite images in Fuerza Aérea Colombiana. With experience in languages such as Python, R, and SQL, they are equipped to explore neural network models and use advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques to create innovative and efficient solutions. With their collaborative and results-oriented approach, this team is well equipped to make significant contributions in their field.
